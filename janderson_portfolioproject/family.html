My Family

 Family is everything to me in a way family makes you who you are and who you aren't.  My house is filled wih lots of chaos, laughter and many memories we have shared together. My family consist of my husband Stephen whom I've been married to for 18 years; 25 all together. We have three wonderful children who are all three very different and unique in their own way!                       

McKenzie

 McKenzie is our oldest she just turned sixteen and in the 11th grade, she got her first car and is in the process of figuring out where she wants to attend college once she graduates. She is truly a daddy's girl all the way and my best friend! McKenzie has a heart of gold and truly makes us very proud as parents we couldn't ask for an easier child. Now the middle child he's the one we worry about!               

Kain 

Kain is our middle child. He is 12 and in the the 7th grade. He's the most like my husband he despises school, refuses to read and can't sit still for a minute. He loves doing anything that involves his hands or racing go karts. Just recently he has become obsessed with fishing which is a nice break from the video games and go kart racing. He's my wild child the one that keeps me as a mom on edge.                   

Lincoln

 Lincoln is the baby he's 8 and going into the 3rd grade. He's our thinker. He wonders about everything and wants to understand the most complex thoughts most adults don't comprehend. He loves to read and if he wants to know how to do something he will sit down and watch videos and  tutorials and by the end of the day he'll have everything all figured out. He's a very unique kid me and my husband still can't figure out how we got him!
